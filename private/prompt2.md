Draft a business proposal for a new subscription-based service that delivers curated artisanal coffee beans to customers' homes. The proposal should include an executive summary, a description of the target market, a competitive analysis, a marketing and sales strategy, and financial projections for the first year. The tone should be professional and persuasive.
