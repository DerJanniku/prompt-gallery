Develop a comprehensive marketing campaign for a new brand of sustainable sneakers. The campaign should target environmentally conscious millennials and Gen Z consumers. Include strategies for social media, influencer collaborations, and content marketing. Outline key messaging, a timeline for the first three months, and suggest metrics for measuring success.
