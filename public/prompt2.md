Write a short story set in a dystopian future where emotions are suppressed by a mandatory daily serum. The protagonist is a government official who secretly stops taking the serum and begins to experience feelings for the first time. Explore their internal conflict and the dangers they face in a society that fears emotion. The story should be between 1,500 and 2,000 words.
